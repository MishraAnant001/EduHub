About the App

Manage your students effortlessly and enjoy a range of additional features to enhance productivity and entertainment. Our application combines student management with essential utilities and fun games to make learning and organization enjoyable for educators, administrators, and students.

Student Management

Administrators can create, view, update, and delete student profiles with our simple CRUD functionality. Students can register and update their own profiles within the system.

Users can try our useful apps like weather app, to-do list, and QR code generator or can also play interesting games like guess the word, stone paper scissor.
